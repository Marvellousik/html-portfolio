My Top Three Movie Recommendations

No 1. Interstellar

In a dystopian future where humanity is lacking survival options, a retired astronaut named Cooper leads a mysterious mission that will take them through a wormhole near Saturn. As they search for a new planet to inhabit, Cooper and his crew must also live with the consequences of leaving home

No 2. Top Gun Mavericks

Thirty years of service leads Maverick to train a group of elite TOPGUN graduates to prepare for a high-profile mission while Maverick battles his past demons.

No 3. The Dark Knight Rises 2012

Bane, an imposing terrorist, attacks Gotham City and disrupts its eight-year-long period of peace. This forces Bruce Wayne to come out of hiding and don the cape and cowl of Batman again.
